Discover Al Reem Island, Abu Dhabi

Top Attractions

Explore the must-visit attractions of Al Reem Island:

  • Reem Central Park: A lush retreat with walking trails, fountains, and playgrounds.
  • Shams Boutik Mall: Your destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment.
  • Snow Abu Dhabi: Among the world's largest indoor snow Parks with 20 rides and attractions.
  • Mangroves: Enjoy kayaking and stand-up paddling and other water sports immersed the beautiful nature of the mangroves.

Demographics

Al Reem Island is a natural island located 600 metres (2,000 ft) off the coast of the Abu Dhabi island.

  • Population: Approximately 100,000 residents.
  • Languages Spoken: Arabic, English, and other international languages.
  • Residential Areas: Mix of luxury apartments, family villas, and studios.
