Site Name

This name represents a quilters guild that is based in Abu Dhabi, the capital city of the UAE.

Site Purpose

This site provides a resource for members of the guild, a place where they can get information about the guild, find out about and sign-up for workshops and other activities that are available, and have access to other guild resources.

Scenarios

  • When and where does the guild meet?

  • What workshops are available and how do I register for them?

  • What size quilt should I make for a queen size bed?

Color Schema

  • Dark Green: #00843d - Text, backgrounds and other elements.

  • Light Green: #e0ffee - backgrounds.

  • Red: #c60101 - text, backgrounds and other elements.

  • White: #ffffff - text and backgrounds.

  • Black: #000000 - text and other elements.

  • Light Camel: #fff2e0 - backgrounds.

Typography

  • Headings Font: Jost

  • Sub-Headings Font: Jost

  • Body text font: Verdana / Geneva / Tahoma
